About Myself

"I don't want to be a product of my environment. I want my environment to be a product of me." (The Departed)

Out of all my academic and professional experiences my passion for solving complex problems in ellegant and novel ways has stuck out above all the rest. My secondary and more applicable passion is studying large chaotic systems. The dynamic nature of how problems appear in these systems is essentially the perfect storm of complexity. Software Engineering is the professional manifestation of my passion and is an effective foothold to impact the world in a meaninful way.

I am an experienced problem solver with a demonstrated history of collaboration as a software engineer, test engineer, data engineer, systems admin, and project manager in the financial services industry. My background in Mathematics has enabled me to learn and apply critical thinking to new situations and logical systems (programming languages for example) with ease.

I have learned that to be an effective problem solver you must fill the gaps in your team so that the optimal solution is attainable. My professional history shows this in the forms of learning new languages quickly, enterprise software solution design, software development, manual and automated test writing, automated code deploy pipelines, team member software installation support, securing confidential data, managing sql server databases, etc.
