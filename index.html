About Myself

Out of all my academic and professional experiences my passion for solving complex problems in elegant and novel ways has stuck out above all the rest. Software Engineering is the professional manifestation of my passions and provides an effective foothold for impacting the world in a meaningful way so that I can make my environment a product of me instead of me a product of it.

I am an experienced problem solver with a demonstrated history of collaboration as a software engineer, test engineer, data engineer, systems admin, and project manager in the financial services industry. My background in Mathematics has enabled me to learn and apply critical thinking to new situations and logical systems (programming languages for example) with ease.

I have learned that to be an effective problem solver you must fill the gaps in your team so that the optimal solution is attainable. My professional history shows this in the forms of learning new languages quickly, enterprise software solution design, software development, manual and automated test writing, automated code deploy pipelines, team member software installation support, securing confidential data, and managing sql server databases, among other things.
